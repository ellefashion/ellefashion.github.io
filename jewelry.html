5 Affordable Jewelry Brands to Bookmark on Instagram

Break up your endless scrolling with some pretty rings and things.

By Madison RexroatPublished: Mar 3, 2023

Fine jewelry’s role in our lives shifted from special occasion-only to everyday wear thanks to a wealth of affordable jewelry brands that abandoned the exclusivity often associated with 14k.

Made with precious metals and encrusted with jewels, fine jewelry is typically still the most expensive form of bauble. But if you’re looking to spend even less, there is also a range of affordable brands offering quality fashion jewelry. For those who don’t know, fashion jewelry is gold-plated over materials like brass, copper, and aluminum. And then there’s demi-fine jewelry—a middle-ground option featuring a sterling silver base and gold plating.

To save you hours of scrolling for the perfect piece within your budget, we compiled our favorite affordable jewelry brands pavé-ing the way—including woman-owned and sustainable labels that are making their mark on the industry. Below, shop our most-liked brands, from updated studs to It-girl chains that fit right in with spring 2023’s latest jewelry trends.

Lilith New York

1 2 3 4

The Vibe: Feminism and Elegance

The Price: Fashion to fine, up to $200

Rather than featuring luxury, the founders have made it their mission to give others refined, gentle and cute pieces of a product line that fit more into the personality and less into the “conventional fashion norms.” The two being the urban women in the fast-paced city, they focused on understanding the need for self-expression that comes with work, dating, partying or simply daily lifestyle. And so, they have launched several product lines styled in a way to complement all these aspects of life. In doing so, they have also managed to keep the prices of their products affordable without compromising on quality or design.

PDPAOLA

View full post on Instagram

The Vibe: Playful and Personalized

The Price: Fashion to fine, up to $2,250

Whether you’re after personalized jewelry or updated classics, PDPaola has a great assortment of pieces to choose from. Charms or chains, dainty or edgy, fine or fashion, the brand is for those who like to put their own spin on jewelry. Choose engravings, letters, charms, and stones to make the pieces personal and up your cool factor. My personal charm combo: my lucky number 7, the labradorite strength charm, and the cute dino charm.

Alex Monroe

View full post on Instagram

The Vibe: Whimsical and Lighthearted

The Price: Fashion to fine, up to $6,850

Inspired by the nature of the English countryside, Alex Monroe jewelry is for those with a sense of whimsy. Creatures of all shapes and sizes (like my favorite beetle necklace), vegetables you would find in any well-kept country garden, and all kinds of trinkets and treasures are what make the brand so unique. Alex Monroe is also committed to sustainable responsibility in the jewelry industry, with pieces being handmade in England with fairmined gold and other responsibly sourced materials.

What people are saying: “Simply delicious. Perfect in every detail. It contains the wonder of nature and summer. Poetic and evocative, I’m in love with it.”

SHOP NOW

Jenny Bird

View full post on Instagram

The Vibe: Cool Girl-Chic

The Price: Fashion, up to $328

A true “cool girl” brand, Jenny Bird keeps things simple and chic with fun twists to spice up your everyday jewelry. From chunky chains and puffy hoops (a personal favorite) to link earrings and mixed metals, the brand’s aesthetic is at the same time modern and heritage-inspired. Think: interesting pieces you’d find in your mom's jewelry box if we were in the year 2050. An extra perk? Most pieces are under $200.

What people are saying: “Beautiful, elegant high quality finish. Definitely elevates your wardrobe.”

The Vibe: Fun AND Fine

The Price: Fine, up to $6,300

Known for its puffy typeface and colorful designs, Type Jewelry offers 14K gold jewelry that’s anything but boring. Rings, earrings, bracelets, and necklaces are all custom-made to look just how you want. Pieces can stand on their own or be stacked together for a maximalist aesthetic that’s sure to stand out. I’m going for a puffy nameplate necklace—which one will you choose?

MAM Originals

The Vibe: Modern and Artistic

The Price: Fashion to fine, up to $4,000

MAM takes modern jewelry to a new level. These pieces are at once works of art and conversation starters, which is exactly what I look for when buying anything new. If you want something modern and simple, yet unique and different, this is where to start. Dangling earring + cuff sets, chunky rings, hand bracelets, and even face jewelry can all be found with this remarkable and exciting brand.
